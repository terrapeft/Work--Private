STATIX

 

The online Statix service enables quick access to detailed global information and easy to read global trading calendars on futures and options contracts, to help improve the efficiency of front and back office operations, without the full cost and risk of maintaining a specialist in-house system. This service helps reduce costs of failed trades, over one third of which are attributed to poor reference data.

 

The content-rich service includes exchange information, holidays, trading sessions, exchange members, contract specifications, trading cycles, first and last trading dates, expiry dates, trading volumes and time zones covering over 100 derivatives exchanges worldwide.

 

Features include:

 

Data from over 100 derivatives exchanges

Exchange information

Exchange member details on over 13,000 exchange members

Contract specifications for over 30,000 contracts

Trading date calendars

Historical volume data

Exchange holiday dates
