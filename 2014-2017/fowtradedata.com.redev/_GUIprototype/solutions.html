SOLUTIONS

 

FOW TRADEDATA provides distinct products and services which are recognised within the industry as both leaders and pathfinders in their respective areas.  It is the primary vendor to many of the top investment banks, including 9 out of 10 of the top tier one global investment banks, the UK regulator (Financial Conduct Authority), Market Data Vendors, Independent Software Vendors, Global ETD Exchanges and other market participants with its data trusted worldwide.

 

FOW TRADEDATA is in the leading edge of smart data engineering, providing data feeds of its industry acclaimed instrument data, enriched with either root level or traded series symbology, mapped to key market data and back office vendor codes.   Ensuring the clean order match of a traded series product to the listed instrument, fundamentally underpins a superior environment for position management.  These enriched feeds allow the import of portfolio transactions from client originated platforms to be instantly mapped into clearing and settlement systems, all looking at the correct exchange traded instrument. Open positions can then be dynamically tracked for clients by utilising the same traded series symbology to retrieve real-time prices from market data connections and reports produced with embellished attribute information.

 

For firms providing brokerage services, offering the seamless implementation of new clients, with invested operations of differing configured technologies for market data and order execution, can provide a competitive advantage in winning that new business, more often than not a switch sale, with minimum disruption and high quality of service.
